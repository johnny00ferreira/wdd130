About Us

Who We Are

Living the Extraordinary is a team of certified guides and river enthusiasts dedicated to creating authentic, professional, and safe rafting experiences. Our mission is to connect people with nature through adventure, teamwork, and respect for the environment.

History

Living the Extraordinary began as a small group of adventurers passionate about rivers and outdoor challenges. Over time, their desire to share this passion evolved into a guided rafting service focused on safety, professionalism, and environmental responsibility.

Officially founded in 2018, the company expanded its equipment, training programs, and guiding certifications. Today, our team continues to offer transformative experiences for individuals, families, and adventure seekers from around the world.
