Living the Extraordinary

About Us

Living the Extraordinary is driven by a clear purpose: to lead people into authentic, safe, and unforgettable adventures powered by the force of nature. Our mission is to deliver rafting experiences that combine excitement, professionalism, and a deep connection to the environment. We are guided by three essential values: safety, respect for nature, and teamwork — pillars that ensure every run is carried out with excellence and responsibility. Our motto captures who we are and what we stand for: “Pushing limits, paddling together.”

History

Living the Extraordinary was born from the dream of a small group of adventurers passionate about rivers, nature, and challenges. In its early years, the team carried out independent expeditions, exploring rapids across the country and studying professional safety and guiding techniques. With every descent, the desire to transform that passion into something greater grew — an experience capable of inspiring others to live beyond the ordinary. In 2018, that dream took shape and Living the Extraordinary was officially founded, with the mission of offering rafting activities that combine adrenaline, safety, and respect for the environment. Since then, the company has expanded its structure, specialized its guides, invested in certified equipment, and strengthened its reputation as a reference in outdoor adventure experiences. Today, Living the Extraordinary remains true to its roots: sharing transformative adventures, connecting people with nature, and showing that with courage and teamwork, anyone can discover the extraordinary within themselves.
