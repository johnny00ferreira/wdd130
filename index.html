Johnny Ferreira Da Silva | WDD 130

Hello, my name is Johnny, and I'll tell you a little about myself. I currently live in the city of João Pessoa in the state of Paraíba. I'm married and 27 years old. I have an amazing daughter. I currently work for an airline called Gol Linhas Aéreas. I love this job, just as I love this city. My favorite places in João Pessoa are the beaches on the south coast; some of them have the meeting of the river and the sea, so sometimes I skip swimming in the sea and go to the river instead. My daughter loves the river. I've been a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for 7 years, and during that same period, I served as a missionary in the best mission in the world, the Brazil Santos Mission.

Paraiba - Brazil

Paraíba is a charming state in the Brazilian Northeast, known for its beautiful beaches and rich cultural heritage. Its capital, João Pessoa, is one of the oldest cities in the country and famous for its sunsets on Jacaré beach. The state is also home to the easternmost point of the Americas, where the sun rises first in Brazil. Beyond its natural beauty, Paraíba stands out for its handicrafts, cuisine, and popular festivals. It's a destination that combines history, nature, and hospitality in every corner.
